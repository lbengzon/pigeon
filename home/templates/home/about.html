About Pigeon

Pigeon Ridesharing is a long-distance carpool matching service that places college students in carpools together based on their destinations.

Currently, we operate on Colgate University's campus helping students find transportation home for major breaks and holidays.

How it works

  • Students at your college list their trip details with us.
  • You request the ride you want through our online platform.
  • We connect you with the driver so you can negotiate the terms of the ride with them directly.
